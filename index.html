Video Content Management System

Flexy offers an out-of-the-box video CMS, where you can upload, add tags and manage publishing all your video and audio on your streaming platforms. You can easily embed your content directly into your websites and apps or create playlists and generate HLS output URLs.

Built-in CDN for Buffer-free Streaming

Deliver a buffer-free streaming experience to your audiences from across the globe with Muvi Flex’s built-in CDN. Powered by AWS CloudFront CDN, publish your content seamlessly and at a faster rate.

Automatic Encoding & Transcoding

Flex’s automatic encoding and transcoding not only reduces your time to market but also optimizes video quality. Uploaded content gets encoded and transcoded automatically into digital format with multiple resolutions from 144p to 4k, to ensure best video resolution for your audiences regardless of their network bandwidth and devices used.

Customizable HTML5-based Online Video Player

Flex gives you the flexibility of customizing the online video player, by adding your logo, watermark or even adding subtitles to your content . You can enhance your brand awareness and audience engagement.

Protection From Piracy

With Flexy, you don’t have to worry about piracy and illegal access to your videos. Muvi Flex’s multi-DRM helps you keep your content safe by offering protection against screen recording, visible and forensic watermarking, etc.

Social Publishing

With Flexy's social publishing, you can not only publish videos on your websites and apps but also on your social media handles right from the CMS. You only need to connect your social media handles with Muvi Flex, select the video, and publish it.

Manage Content Access

Flexy’s geo-blocking lets you manage the streaming access of your content effortlessly across the globe. Set restrictions based on geographical location, based on- country, region and city. You can white-list and black-list IP addresses of any region anytime from the CMS as per your distribution strategy.

Analytics and Reports

Flexy enables you to track and assess the performance of your videos on websites and apps. With analytics and reports, you will get detailed insights into your video performance such as user behavior, devices used, drop-off rates, and more.
